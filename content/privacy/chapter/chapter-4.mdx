### 4. Who We Share Your Information With

To provide you with the service, and as described in Section 3 of this Privacy Policy, we need to share some information about you with others. Information collected or accessed on the Site may be shared with entities both within and outside of our corporate family. Please note that any third party with whom we share your personal information will be legally and/or contractually required to provide the same or equal protection to your personal data as we outline in this Privacy Policy. We may share your information with the following entities:

1. ##### Payers with Whom You Interact:

    We may share information about you with Payers with whom you interact while using our Service.
2. ##### Financial Institutions:

    We may share your personal information with the Financial Institutions and affiliates.
3. ##### Our Service Providers and Other Third-Parties:

     We may share your information with our service providers and other trusted third parties such as customer support service centers, advertising networks, analytics tools providers, and software development kit providers we hire to help with our business operations (including, but not limited to, providing customer service, verifying your identity, preventing and detecting fraudulent activity, analytics, marketing as permissible, operations, and enforcing of our terms and conditions).
4. ##### Other Entities with Your Consent:

    We may share your personal information with other third parties to whom you explicitly ask us to send your information (or about whom you are otherwise explicitly notified and consent to when using a specific service). On the Site, you may have opportunities to express interest in or register for other services. If you do, we will provide information about you to those third-parties, or parties working on their behalf, to facilitate your request.
5. ##### Law Enforcement, Government Agencies and, Other Authorized Third Parties:

    We may share information about you, as permitted by law, with law enforcement, governmental agencies, and other authorized third-parties.
6. ##### Change of Control - New Owners:

    We may share your information with other business entities, should we plan to merge with or be acquired by that business entity. Should this occur, we will make reasonable efforts to request that the new combined entity follow this Privacy Policy with respect to your personal information. If your personal information would be used contrary to this Privacy Policy, we will request that the new owner provide you with prior notice.
7. ##### Ad Networks:

    On our Site, we may allow third parties to provide analytics services and serve Payall advertisements on our behalf. These entities may use their own cookies, web beacons, device identifiers, and other technologies to collect information about your use of the Site, including your IP address, web browser, internet service provider information, the website you came from, pages viewed, time spent on pages or in apps, links clicked, and conversion information. This information may be used by us and others to, among other things, analyze and track data, determine the popularity of certain content, and deliver Payall advertising on our Site and other websites. The use of these technologies by these third parties is subject to their own privacy policies and is not covered by this Privacy Policy. For more information about third-party ad servers at our Site and your ability to opt-out of targeted advertising from such third parties, please visit our [Cookies Policy](/cookies-policy).
