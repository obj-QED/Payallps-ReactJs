### 12. Submissions, Postings and E-mails

***Payall*** is interested in hearing from you regarding your questions or comments about our Site. However, ***Payall*** does not accept or consider unsolicited submissions of any kind (e.g. ideas, treatments, concepts, or any other materials) in any format, by any means of transmission (including email). Any such submissions are either returned to the sender without being reviewed or deleted or discarded without being reviewed. Therefore, please do not send any unsolicited submissions to any member of ***Payall***.
