### 18. Security

Any usernames and passwords used for this Site are for individual use only. You shall be responsible for the security of your username and password (if any). Payall shall be entitled to monitor your username and password and, at its discretion, require you to change it. If you use a username and password that Payall considers insecure, Payall will be entitled to require this to be changed and/or terminate your account.
